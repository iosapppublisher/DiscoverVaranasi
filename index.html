Privacy Policy for Discover Varanasi iOS Application

Last Updated: 08 Feb 2024

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of our 'Discover Varanasi'. We respect your privacy and do not gather or store any personal data.

2. Permissions

To enhance your experience and enable certain functionalities within the Discover Varanasi app, we require access to specific permissions on your device. These include:

Camera: Allows you to capture and share photos of your experiences in Varanasi directly through the app.
Photo Gallery: Enables you to select and share photos from your device, fostering a community-driven sharing of experiences and memories.
Location Services: Assists in providing location-based recommendations, navigation, and relevant event information, enriching your exploration of Varanasi.

3. Updates and Data Security

We may release updates to improve the app's functionality or address issues. These updates do not compromise your privacy or require additional data collection.

All the information provided within the Discover Varanasi app is based on our knowledge and extensive research to offer accurate, up-to-date content. However, we rely on a variety of sources for the information we share, and while we strive to ensure the reliability and accuracy of the information presented, we cannot guarantee its absolute accuracy. Information within the app is provided with the best of intentions and is meant for general guidance and informational purposes only.

We encourage users to verify the information independently, especially before making any decisions based on the information found in the Discover Varanasi App. We welcome feedback and corrections from our users to improve the accuracy and usefulness of the content we provide.
